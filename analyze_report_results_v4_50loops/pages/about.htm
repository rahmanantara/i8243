The STDF Examinator is a free software tool from Galaxy. This product is royalty free, and does
not require a maintenance contract! You can run this software as long as you respect the
license terms. We recommend that you periodically visit our web site for the latest release! Our
engineers keep adding new must have features! Don't forget it is free, so just visit our web site to
get the latest version

Understanding the Release expiration date: you may have noticed that the STDF Examinator
About box displays an expiration date. This behavior has been designed to ensure you will visit our
web site in the near future to get the release updates, and benefit from all the improvements! If you
don't have easy access to the Internet, ask someone else to download the release for you ... this is
not a license breach as long as you notify us by email once you have installed the new release
(simply send us an email with your name, company name, and company Email address). Although
the STDF examinator software is free, Galaxy still incurs a significant cost for its enhancement and
support. We believe this expiration date is a fair mechanism to allow us keep an accurate database
of users, while letting you enjoy a free, powerful, and easy to use STDF tool. The expiration date 
typically runs for 6 months to 1 year from the date it is released to our web site...so the worst-case
scenario is that you will have to download two new releases in a year
